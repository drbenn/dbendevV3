More Than Last Time
MTLT Fitness Tracker. A play on Greg Doucette's Harder Than Last Time mantra. I was tired of tracking gym workouts in Google Sheets, with my burgeoning Angular skills, thought I would tackle a new full-stack project that included NGXS state management, NestJS and Mongoose. Now while working out I can easily see the performance of my last attempt and best attempt without scrambling through a spreadsheet. Minimum viable alpha Released August 24, 2022 and updates ongoing.
